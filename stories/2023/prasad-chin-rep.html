short general

To The District Collector Anakapalli

Sir, Sub: BARC LANDS- Anakapalli District - Atchutapuram Mandal- Joganna Palem Village Payment of Land Compensation to the assigned lands taken for BARC purposes- Representation of PVSRK Prasad - Reg.

***

I submit that I am an Ex-Servicemen (Air Force). I submit that I was assigned Government land admeasuring Ac.3.04 Cts in S.No.168/1 Joganna Palem Village of Atchutapuram Mandal in the year 1980 and I was granted D.Patta to that effect by the erstwhile Tahsildar Elamanchili in DC No. 180/85/A1/Dated 20-1-1981. I have been in peaceful possession of the land ever since.

In the year 2004, the APPIC/BARC acquired the lands in the Atchutapuram Mandal for its works and my land was taken over by the APIIC for BARC promising that I would be paid Land compensation for the land taken over by the Government.

Ever since I have submitted several representations to the District Collector and other Revenue Officials in the erstwhile Visakhapatnam District and all the Officers have verified my case and I learnt that proposals were also sent to the Government for payment of compensation to me along with others.

I also submit that my previous Employer BARC where I worked as Assistant Security Officer have also recommended my case to the District Collector Visakhapatnam for payment of compensation.

Consequent to the formation of Anakapalli District, I approached the Office to enquire about my case with regard to payment of compensation and I am advised to submit a fresh application in the new Anakapalli District.

I enclose herewith copies of my previous representations in regard to payment of compensation and I earnestly request the District Collector Anakapalli to kindly consider my case for payment of land Compensation and arrange payment at the earliest as I am getting old (71) years.

Yours faithfully

Encl: PVSRK PRASAD MY ADDRESS
